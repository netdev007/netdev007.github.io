Upselling

After Hours Scripts | Mandatory Sales Script

When up-selling to a customer, always begin with Premium Plus!

·        “Mr./Ms. ­_____________ , now that you have experienced the Tech Support 360 Service first hand, let me review the benefits of a Tech Support 360 subscription.

·        Tech Support 360 offers a wide variety of services covering all of your computer needs.  With a Tech Support 360 subscription you have access to unlimited remote support, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with one of our certified, US based technicians.  You can schedule service at any time, either over the telephone or on our website.

·        The Tech Support 360 Premium Plus subscription is the best value.  With Premium Plus we offer:

o   Unlimited PC Tune Ups.  That means that should you experience slow response time or pop-up windows, suggesting your computer may be infected with a virus, we can perform a cleaning and tune up at any time.

o   With Premium Plus, we also offer unlimited data backup.  This means that in the event of an outage you don’t need to worry about losing your data as we can recover and restore all lost files at any time.

o   With this subscription we also support your software and networking needs as well as unlimited peripherals.

o   The unlimited peripheral support is great because we can help set up your printers, Blackberrys and Smartphones, iPods, digital cameras, and other devices you need to run your business.

o   This subscription is a great deal at $28 per month (per computer) for a one year subscription.”

If the customer is still reluctant, make one more attempt to up-sell them into an Advanced subscription:

·        “Mr./Ms. __________________ , we also offer a less comprehensive subscription, the Tech 360 Advanced subscription, for the low price of $19 per month (per computer) for a 12 month subscription.

·        The AT&T Tech Support 360 Advanced subscription provides basic computer support services.

·        Our Advanced subscription includes hardware support, assistance with the operating system, software applications and setting up 2 peripherals.

·        This subscription does not include the unlimited virus removal, software installation or removal, ongoing tune ups, wireless network support, or data backup.”

 

If the customer is reluctant to purchase a Tech 360 sub; or if they came in on a sub without backup, offer the Backup and Go subscription.

·        “Mr./Ms. __________________ , I notice that you are not currently running a back- up solution on your computer.  As you know, in the event of an outage you are in danger of losing all your data.

·        Tech Support 360 offers an inexpensive back-up solution, Backup and Go, which will protect your computer in such an event.  The solution essentially keeps your files backed up over the internet, so in the event that you lose your data, all protected files may be retrieved quickly and easily by one of our technicians.

·        If you wish, I can install Backup and Go on your computer today for the low cost of $7 per month.”
